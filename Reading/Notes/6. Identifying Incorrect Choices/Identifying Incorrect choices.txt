A. Uses the same words as text
: Bila ada kata-kata yang sama dengan text yang dibaca. Kita harus
koreksi kembali jawaban tersebut.
    
    A.1. Contoh
    : Lihat(images/uses-the-same-words.png). Pada jawaban C
    Went extinct about 160 million years ago, kita lihat pada text reading
    lived around 161 million years ago. Punya arti yang berbeda.
      Pada jawaban D 
    Were discovered by scientist in 1890. Kita lihat pada text reading
    "They were first classified as seperate from other dinosaur species in 1890"
    Memiliki arti berbeda. 

B. Includes the adverbs all,always, never
: Bila jawaban mengandung kata keterangan seperti "all", "always", atau "never" kita
harus curiga dikarenakan biasanya text ilmiah menggunakan kata "usually" atau "most of the times"

    B.1. Contoh
    : Lihat (images/include-all,alaways,never.png) Pada jawaban C 
    "The evidence shows ceratopsians of all ages and genders lived, ate, and
    slept in groups" Kurang tepat karena "Suggest that many, if not all
    species lived together in herds"

C. Contains the wrong modifier
: - The event has had tremendous impact
  - The event has had some impact
  - The event has had almost no impact
  Ketiga kalimat tersebut berbicara tentang akibat acara. Tetapi dibedakan oleh
  modifiernya. Kita harus teliti melihat modifier jawaban.

  C.1. Time Modifiers
  : - Almost two decades ago (20 years)
    - the early 18th century (1700-1725)
    - the late 1900s (1995-1999)
